Getting Started (3 Steps)

  1. Fill in Your Information

    Start with your university name and basic course information. Click section headers to expand/collapse. All fields are optional - use only what you need!

  2. Preview Your Syllabus

    Click "Update Preview" to see your syllabus in real-time. The preview shows exactly how your final document will look.

  3. Export Your Work

    Click "Export to Word" for an editable .doc file, or "Export to PDF" for a print-ready document.

Pro Tips

  • Save Often: Click "Save Draft" to save your work locally in your browser
  • Customize Schedule: Use the schedule builder to add as many weeks/modules/units as your course needs
  • Use Placeholders: Gray placeholder text provides examples and guidance
  • Learning Outcomes: Start each outcome with "Students will be able to..." and use action verbs (analyze, evaluate, create)
  • Leave Blank Fields: Only fill in sections relevant to your course - blank fields won't appear in the final syllabus

Time Estimates

  • New Syllabus: 30-45 minutes for a comprehensive document
  • Update Existing: 15-20 minutes to revise dates and policies
  • Quick Version: 10-15 minutes for essential information only

Recommended Workflow

  1. University Name & Basic Course Information (5 min)
  2. Instructor Information (5 min)
  3. Course Description & Learning Outcomes (10 min)
  4. Assessment & Grading (10 min)
  5. Course Schedule (10 min)
  6. Policies (5-10 min)
  7. Review & Export (5 min)
